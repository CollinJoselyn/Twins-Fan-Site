Playoff Appearances

2002

In 2002 the Twins made it to the playoffs for the first time since 1991. The twins were almost contracted that year. They played the heavily favored Oakland Athletics, who won 103 games. The Twins were the youngest team in the league that year. They beat the Athletics in 5 games and advanced to the ALCS to face the angels. They would lose to the Angels in 5 games.

2003

2003 was the beginning of the Twins and Yankees rivalry. If you can call it that, since the Yankees always end up winning. The Twins won the first game of the series, then lost the last three.

2004

2004 ALDS was just like the 2003 series. The Twins would lose in four games to the Yankees. Game 1 went great for the Twins, Johan Santana threw a complete game shutout. Game 2 the Twins lost a close game that went into extra innings. Game 3 did not go well they struggled on both sides of the ball. Game 4 went well until the 8th inning. Johan Santana threw 7 shutout innings and was brought out of the game for Juan Rincon. Bernie williams hit a RBI single to score a run. The four run 8th inning for the Yankees brought pure silence into the metrodome. Then later that inning with 2 players on base, Reuben Sierra hit a three run home run to tie the game. Then later on the Twins would go on to lose in the 11th inning and be eliminated from the playoffs.

2006

2006 was suppose to be the year the Twins win the world series. They had Johan Santana, a CY young award winner. Justin Morneau the MVP winner and Joe mauer the batting champ. We had all the pieces in place. We were facing the Oakland athletics and we had home field advantage. We completely choked. The twins ended up getting swepted. This would go down as one of the most disappointing moments in Minnesota sports history.

2009

2009 the Twins barely made it to the playoffs. They were seven games out of first in the beginning of September. They forged a huge comeback and beat the Detroit Tigers in a one game playoff, which was the greatest game I have ever watched. The Twins again got sweeped by the Yankees. Got blown out the first game. The second game Joe Nathan blew a save in the nineth inning by giving up a homerun to Alex Rodriguez. Then in the 12th inning Mark texeria hit a walk off homerun. Then in game 3 the twins lost 4 to 1.

2010

2010 was another disappointing series againt New York. The Twins had home field advantage and had a great offensive team. But mediore pitching came back to bite us. Game one we had a three run lead going into the fifth inning. Franscico Liriano was throwing a shutout. All was going well but then the Yankees scored four runs effortlessly. This knocked the wind out of me and all Twins fans sails. We thought things would be different this year but instead it was all the same. Twins got sweeped by the Yankees for the second year in a row.
