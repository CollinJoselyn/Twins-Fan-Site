About Me

Hello, I'm Collin.I am a student at Metropolitan State University studying computer information technology. I have been a big Twins fan all my life. I grew up watching lots of games. I always keep up with what's going on with the team.

My favorite players growing up were Joe Mauer, Torii Hunter, Justin Morneau, and Johan Santana. My favorite current players are Eddie Rosario, Byron Buxton, and Miguel Sano.

The best Twins game I've ever watched was the 2009 AL central tiebreaker against the Detroit Tigers. The most memerable seasons for me was 2009 and 2017.
