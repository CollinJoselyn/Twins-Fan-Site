Twins offseason needs

By: Collin Joselyn

The Twins are coming off a pretty successful year. After losing 103 games in 2016, they turned it around and made it to the playoffs as the second wildcard team. Despite losing to the Yankees again, Twins fans are excited about this team again. Offensively the team was terrific. This roster is full of good young position players. But the biggest hole in this roster is the starting pitching. The Twins have been lacking a strong pitching ace since they traded Johan Santana. For free agents the Twins should try to go for Yu Darvish. Darvish has been a good pitcher for the Rangers and Dodgers. He posted an impressive WHIP of 1.16 this year. Another free agent pitcher that would be a good addition would be Alex Cobb. Cobb in 2017 had an ERA of 3.66 and a WHIP of 1.22. The Twins should also consider trading for a pitcher. Their possible options would be Chris Archer of the Rays. Archer last year posted a WHIP of 1.25. If the Twins want to be serious contenders that can go past the opening round, they will need a good starting pitcher added to the roster.
